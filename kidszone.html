Kidz Zone


Passport to Scotland Tent is your child’s entrance to the fascinating world of Scottish Heritage and Tradition. Children can pick up a “Scottish Passport” and as they explore the various clans of Scotland they can have clan representatives stamp it. A special token awaits each child who completes the stamp requirement.


Come meet and play games with your hometown curlers in the Kid’s Zone!

Lassies' Games

Sunday afternoon on the competition field Coordinated by Clan Keith. Competitions will include: The Haggis Hurl for Distance, The Haggis Hurl for Accuracy, Tossing the Broom, and the Tattie & Spoon Race Competitors in each event will be divided into age groups determined by the number of and ages of entrants. Medals will be awarded to winners in each age category - number of medal winners in each event will be based on the number of entrants.
