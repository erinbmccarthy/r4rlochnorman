Whisky Tasting


The Rural Hill Scotch Whisky Seminar Presented by McIntosh Law Firm




The Rural Hill Scotch Whisky Seminar Presented by McIntosh Law Firm exposes participants to the world of Scotch Whisky by allowing participants to taste and explore several different varieties. The tasting selection is different each year, but in years past we have enjoyed various years and bottlings of Famous Grouse, Ardbeg, Monkey Shoulder, Black Bottle, Glenlivet, Macallan, Glenmorangie, Highland Park, Talisker, Clynelish, Glenkinchie and more! Always informative, never stuffy or boring, the Loch Norman Highland Games wouldn’t be the same without these great seminars!

Returning for 2018!

In conjunction with our outstanding Scotch Whisky seminars being presented at 1:30 both Saturday and Sunday, Rural Hill is proud to present:

Whiskies of the World!

Presented by established experts on all things whiskey (and whisky.) Whiskies of the world ticket holders will be treated to an educational and delicious seminar comparing and contrasting several varieties of American, Irish, Japanese, Canadian, and Scottish whiskies! Seminar will be held at 3:30 Saturday inside the Rural Hill Cultural Center. Each ticket purchaser will receive a limited edition tasting glass provided by the McIntosh Law Firm! Scotch tastings will be different each day, so feel free to attend all both seminars for the ultimate experience! A huge thanks to the fine folks at the McIntosh Law Firm for presenting these great seminars to the Loch Norman Highland Games!




Other Beverages

North Carolina Beer, North Carolina Wine, Coffee, and bottled water are available throughout the day. Many of our food vendors will sell soft drinks as well. Please note that “brown bagging” and outside alcohol are strictly prohibited at this event.
